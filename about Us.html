About Us

Laxmi Narain dubey College was started in the year 1966 in the district head quarter of East-Champran, Motihari.To full fill the educational needs of this backward district. It is situated in the head of town just in front of Sadar Hospital and Town Thana. It imparts teaching right from Intermediate level to Honors level in every subject of Science and Art’s . This has a unit of Bachelor in Computer application ( B.C.A. ) teaching and a centre for Distance Education of B.R.A. Bihar University, Muzaffarpur. The College was made a CONSTITUENT UNIT in 1980. The College is well reputed for its Teaching, Discipline, and Fairness in Examination. It is approved by the University Grants Commission, New Delhi under section 2(f) of the U. G. C. Act, 1956. The College was established in 1970 and affiliated permanently in 1980 by the Bihar University, Muzaffarpur (Bihar).More than 3,000 students are reading in the college, comprising OBC, SC, ST, PH, Minorities & General Categories. Results are praiseworthy because many of them have ranked in the University. The College awards each year a number of prizes to students for high achievement in academic & extra-curricular activities. Scholarships and other financial aid are given to deserving students on the basis of merit. The College provides a liberal, lively and competitive environment enabling students to carve out a niche for themselves in their chosen arenas. Besides the infrastructural facilities, the strength of the College lies in its congenial and enriching atmosphere, which plays a crucial role in maintaining the excellent record the College is renowned for.Generations of students who have been groomed in this College have excelled in different walks of life, with the years spent in the College providing them a solid foundation on which they could craft their success. The College is committed to providing the leadership and direction for the times ahead in every sphere of human enterprise and endeavour.
